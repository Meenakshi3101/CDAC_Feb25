4) Why isn’t it possible to see the first five rows of a table?

ANS :It is possible to see first 5 rows of a column by using, LIMIT keyword in SELECT statement. 
Statement "SELECT * FROM table LIMIT 5;" will help to see first 5 rows of a table.
